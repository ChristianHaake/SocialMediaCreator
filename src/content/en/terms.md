## Educational purpose

SocialMediaCreator creates fictional digital communication scenarios for
education, media literacy, and classroom use. It is not a real social-media
platform.

## User responsibility

Users are responsible for entered content, images, and distribution of
exports. They must respect personality rights, copyright, privacy, school
rules, and applicable law.

## Prohibited use

The following is prohibited:

- identity misuse or imitation of real people without permission
- cyberbullying, harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- defamation or deliberate humiliation
- disinformation, deception, or presenting simulations as genuine evidence
- publication of private messages or personal data
- infringement of copyright, trademarks, or personality rights
- unlawful, commercial, or harmful use
- removing or concealing the automatic export label

## Exported content

Exports are visibly labeled as simulations. The label does not remove user
responsibility. Files must remain within the agreed educational context and
must not be distributed deceptively.

The application is provided without a guarantee of availability or suitability
for a particular purpose. Educational and legal information does not replace
case-specific advice.
